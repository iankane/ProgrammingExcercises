To capture the "p" matrix, press the "p" key.

To capture the "q" matrix, press the "q" key.

To reset the matrix to the starting position, press the "r" key.

To run the slerp equation up by .1, press the "t" key.

To run the slerp equation down by .1, press the "T" key.
